How do I Apply for a Hostel?

  1. Click on the Apply link on the side bar


  2. Fill in the hostel application form accurately and click on the apply button


  3. You will receive an email with your request code, make sure you write it down.
  4. Proceed to pay the required amount at the finance office and take the proof of payment to the housing department office.
  5. Once your payment is verified you will receive a confirmation email with the details of your accomodation

  6. Ensure that you pay the housing amount within 6 working days or your request will be discarded, in which case you will have to apply again.

How do I check if my application has been accepted / declined?

  1. Click on the search link on the on the side bar


  2. Type in the request number you received via email and click search
  3. The Request Number, Status and Comments are displayed below the search form


How do I choose a preferred room and hostel?

The portal automatically selects a room and hostel for you once you apply.

Where do I collect Keys?

You can collect the keys from the housing department once your request has been approved.

What do I do if my application status says pending?

This is an indicator that you have not notified the housing department that you have paid. Kindly visit th housing department with your request code and proof of payment to claim a room in the hostels.

What do I do if my application status says Declined? / I Can't find my request?

This is an indicator that your request has been declined. Kindly apply again.
